Making healthy choices isn't always easy. It can be hard to find the time and energy to exercise regularly or prepare healthy meals. However, your efforts will pay off in many ways, and for the rest of your life. We can adopt healthy habits to maintain good health.

Benefits of a Healthy Lifestyle:

  • Increased energy levels
  • Better mental health
  • Reduced risk of chronic diseases
  • Improved quality of life

Healthy Habits to Consider:

  1. Regular exercise
  2. Balanced diet
  3. Adequate sleep
  4. Stress management

Nutrients

Nutrients are substances required by the body to perform its basic functions. Nutrients must be obtained from our diet since the human body cannot make them. Nutrients have one or more of three basic functions: they provide energy, contribute to body structure, and/or regulate chemical processes in the body.

Benefits of Nutrients:

  • Energy Production
  • Cell Growth and Repair
  • Immune System Support
  • Healthy Skin, Hair, and Nails
  • Weight Management

Fitness

"Fitness" is a broad term that means something different to each person, but it refers to your own optimal health and overall well-being. Being fit not only means physical health, but emotional and mental health, too. It defines every aspect of your health. Smart eating and active living are fundamental to fitness.

Benefits of Fitness:

  • Improved Cardiovascular Health
  • Muscle Strength and Endurance
  • Better Balance and Coordination
  • Enhanced Flexibility
  • Stress Reduction

Sports

'Sport' is understood to mean all forms of physical activity that contribute to physical fitness, mental well-being and social interaction. A 'Game' is a physical or mental activity whose only purpose is the pleasure obtained from practising it. Sports also offer an enjoyable, exciting environment in which to learn how to handle both failure and success.

Benefits of Sports:

  • Mental Toughness
  • Improved Concentration and Focus
  • Mental Toughness
  • Cardiovascular Health

Gym

A gymnasium, also known as a gym, is a covered location for athletics. The word is derived from the ancient Greek term "gymnasium". They are commonly found in athletic and fitness centres, and as activity and learning spaces in educational institutions.

Benefits of Gym:

  • Improved Physical Fitness
  • Improved Flexibility
  • Better Sleep
  • Muscle Growth and Strength
